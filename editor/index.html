Who We Are

A church that cares

We believe every person has purpose, every family deserves support, and every community can thrive. These three values shape everything we do.

🙏

Authentic Faith

Deep, honest, Spirit-led worship every week — no performance, just presence.

❤️

Genuine Community

Small groups, pastoral care, and a culture where nobody walks alone.

🌍

Local Impact

Feeding programmes, school support, and community development right here in Chitungwiza.

Lead Pastor

A message from Pastor David Nhari

We started Maranatha with eleven people in a living room. Today we are over two thousand strong — but the heart has never changed. Every Sunday we gather as family, and every week we push outwards into the community we love.

I believe the local church is the hope of Zimbabwe. Come and be part of something bigger than yourself.
